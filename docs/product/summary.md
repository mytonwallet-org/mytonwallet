# MyTonWallet — Product Summary

MyTonWallet is a cryptocurrency wallet that lets you store, send, receive, swap, and stake digital assets on the **TON**, **TRON**, and **Solana** blockchains — all from a single app.

Available on **iOS**, **Android**, **Web**, as a **browser extension** (Chrome, Firefox, Opera), a **desktop app** (Windows, macOS, Linux), and a **Telegram Mini App**.

---

## Getting Started

When you open MyTonWallet for the first time, you can either **create a new wallet** or **import an existing one**. Creating a wallet generates a secret recovery phrase (12 or 24 words) that you must write down and store safely — it's the only way to recover your wallet if you lose access to your device.

You can also import a wallet using a recovery phrase, a private key, or by connecting a **Ledger hardware wallet**. If you just want to watch an address without managing it, you can add it as a **view-only account**.

Once inside, you'll set up a **password** or **PIN** to protect your wallet. You can also enable **biometric unlock** — Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint — for quick access. The app locks automatically after a configurable period of inactivity.

You can create **multiple accounts** within one app — useful if you want to separate personal and business funds, or manage wallets on different blockchains.

## Your Portfolio

The home screen shows your **total balance** across all tokens and chains, with price changes over the last 24 hours. Tap any token to see a **price chart** (from 1 day to all-time) and your transaction history for that specific asset.

MyTonWallet supports the native tokens of each chain — **TON**, **TRX**, and **SOL** — as well as thousands of additional tokens (Jettons on TON, TRC-20 on TRON, SPL on Solana). You can also **import tokens** by their contract address if they aren't listed by default.

Customize your view by **pinning** your favorite tokens to the top, **hiding** ones you don't use, and choosing your preferred **display currency** (USD, EUR, RUB, and others).

---

## Sending & Receiving

To **send** crypto, enter the recipient's address (or scan a **QR code**), choose the token and amount, and confirm. The app shows you the estimated **network fee** before you send. On TON, you can also send to human-readable **DNS domains** (like `alice.ton`) instead of long addresses, and attach a **comment** to your transfer — optionally encrypted so only the recipient can read it.

For power users, **multi-send** lets you batch multiple transfers at once by uploading a CSV file.

To **receive**, simply share your wallet address or the generated **QR code**. You can also create an **invoice** with a pre-filled amount to make it easy for someone to pay you the exact right amount.

---

## Swapping Tokens

You can **swap one token for another** directly inside the wallet. MyTonWallet compares rates across multiple decentralized exchanges (DEXes) and picks the best one for you. For tokens on different blockchains, **cross-chain swaps** are available through an integrated exchange (Changelly).

The swap screen shows you the exchange rate, price impact, and all fees before you confirm — so there are no surprises.

---

## NFTs & Collectibles

Your **NFTs** appear in a gallery organized by collection. This includes regular NFT art, **Telegram Gifts**, TON DNS domains, and more. You can view each item's attributes, send NFTs to other addresses, or even transfer multiple at once in bulk.

There are also **MTW Cards** — special branded collectibles you can mint directly from the app and use as your **wallet card background**.

---

## Staking & Earning

If you hold TON, you can **stake** it to earn passive rewards. MyTonWallet offers multiple staking pools to choose from, including **liquid staking** (where you receive a liquid token representing your stake and can still use your funds) and **nominators staking**.

The staking screen shows your current balance, accumulated rewards, and estimated APY. You can **claim rewards** or **unstake** at any time (subject to a short cooldown period depending on the pool).

---

## Exploring dApps

The **Explore** tab is your gateway to decentralized applications. Browse curated categories, search for specific dApps, or check out featured picks. On mobile, dApps open in an **in-app browser** — no need to leave the wallet. On other platforms, they open in your regular browser or connect directly through the extension.

dApps connect to your wallet via **TON Connect** (for TON dApps) or **WalletConnect** (for TRON, Solana, and other chains). When a dApp requests a transaction, you'll see a **preview of exactly what it wants to do** before you approve — including the estimated fees. You stay in control: you can review, confirm, or reject each request.

## Buying & Selling Crypto

You can **buy crypto with a bank card** or **sell crypto back to fiat** through an integrated Moonpay widget, without leaving the app. Availability depends on your region.

---

## TON DNS Domains

If you own a **TON DNS domain** (like `yourname.ton`), it shows up in your wallet alongside your NFTs. You can **link** it to your wallet address so people can send you TON just by typing your domain name. The app warns you when a domain is approaching its **expiration date** so you can renew it in time.

## Security & Safety

MyTonWallet is designed to keep your funds safe:

- Your **recovery phrase and private keys** are encrypted and stored locally on your device — they are never sent to any server.
- **Scam detection** warns you when an address is flagged as suspicious, and alerts you about lookalike domain names.
- You can **hide sensitive data** (balances, addresses) on screen when you're in public.
- **Back up your wallet** at any time by viewing your secret words or exporting your private key — the app walks you through the process and verifies you've saved everything correctly.
- **Biometric authentication** (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint) is available across platforms for an extra layer of protection beyond your password.
- On desktop, secrets are stored in the **OS keychain** (macOS Keychain, Windows Credential Manager).

## Activity & History

Every transaction you make — sends, receives, swaps, staking operations — appears in your **activity feed**. You can see the full details of each transaction, track **pending** transfers in real time, and filter by chain or token. Each token's detail page also has its own activity history so you can quickly see what happened with a specific asset.
